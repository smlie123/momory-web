Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

ChatKeeper is a browser extension designed to help users navigate, save, organize, and export ChatGPT conversations. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how ChatKeeper handles user data.

1. Data Collection

ChatKeeper does not collect, transmit, sell, or process any personal data on external servers.

The extension does not collect:

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Browsing history outside of ChatGPT pages
  • Search queries
  • Account credentials or authentication data
  • Analytics, tracking, or advertising data

2. Local Data Storage

ChatKeeper stores user-saved ChatGPT conversations locally in the browser using technologies such as IndexedDB.

This data:

  • Remains entirely on your device
  • Is never uploaded to any remote server
  • Is only accessible within your browser profile

3. Data Usage

Locally stored data is used solely to provide ChatKeeper’s core functionality, including:

  • Displaying a conversation outline on ChatGPT pages
  • Allowing users to save ChatGPT conversations
  • Organizing saved conversations into custom categories
  • Browsing saved conversations in the home view
  • Exporting conversations as PDF or Markdown files

4. Data Sharing

ChatKeeper does not share user data with third parties.

No analytics services, tracking scripts, advertising platforms, or third-party SDKs are used.

5. Permissions

ChatKeeper requests certain browser permissions strictly to enable user-facing features. These permissions are used only when the user interacts with the extension.

  • Active tab access – to read visible ChatGPT conversation content and generate a conversation outline
  • Host permissions (ChatGPT domains only) – to inject UI elements and enable navigation and saving features on ChatGPT pages
  • Storage permissions – to save conversations locally in the browser

6. Data Retention

All data remains stored locally until the user chooses to delete it or removes the extension from their browser.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how ChatKeeper handles data, you may contact the developer at wangjiasansan@gmail.com.

ChatKeeper is a local-first tool. Your ChatGPT conversations stay with you.
